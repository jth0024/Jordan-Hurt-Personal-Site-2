Experience

I worked primarily with CAD software during this internship, but the experience helped to prepare me for a life in front of the computer. I started college after that internship, and my recent CAD experience helped me to earn a co-op position with SAIC. I started in the 3D modeling department, but after a few months I wanted to try something new. So, I transfered to a software department and began working on a mobile web application. I was instantly hooked, and I knew that I had to pursue a career in web development.

As a result of my newfound interest in software, I decided to gain as much development experience as possible during my remaining semesters at Auburn. I finished co-op work during my junior year, and I decided to take on a Computer Science minor that semester. The minor delayed graduation an extra semester, but luckily, it also led me to my current position as a UX Analyst at Intergraph.
