About

Design

We will discuss project requirements and design goals, and I'll create functional prototypes using cutting-edge tools. I care about my designs, so I will tweak the details until everything is just right. The process is only finished when we are both completely satisfied.

Develop

I build front-end sites using a combination of HTML5, JavaScript, and CSS3. Using popular front-end frameworks, I will create powerful and effective experiences for your users. If necessary, I will collaborate with experienced back-end developers to implement more complicated functionality.

Mobile First

A great website should look great on every screen. I understand the importance of mobile, so I build responsive websites that look fantastic on your smartphone, but scale to take advantage of your fancy monitor too.

User-Centered

Websites are powerful, but that doesn't mean they have to be complicated. A good site should be simple and intuitive. As a UX Analyst, it's my job to assess the needs of users and create simple, yet powerful, experiences.

Truly Inspired

If you aren't inspired, why bother? Anyone can build a carbon-copy website, but it takes an inspired mind to deliver great results. I strive to build experiences that are cohesive, thematic, and delightfully different from the rest.
